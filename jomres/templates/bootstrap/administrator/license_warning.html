Plugins greatly extend the functionality of the system, and it's through sales of Download and Support licenses that we finance future development of Jomres.

If you would like to get access to any of the plugins listed in the Jomres plugin manager, or use our support service, we would ask that you consider purchasing a Download and Support license.

You can still help!

It won't get you any support or plugins, but you'll definately feel better about yourself ;)

If you don't want to donate to us, we'd ask if you would consider instead making a donation to the Dog's Trust UK, our chosen Charity. You can donate to them either directly on their site, or via Paypal.
